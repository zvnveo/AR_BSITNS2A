   The objective of this research paper is to explore the benefits and applications of Augmented Reality (AR) in the fields of education and gaming. In particular, this paper aims to explore how the use of AR can improve students' learning experience and improve their engagement in the educational environment. This research also explores the potential of AR in games, analyzing how this technology can provide players with an immersive experience and increase interactivity. Through an in-depth review of the literature, this paper will provide an objective analysis of the advantages of AR in education and gaming, considering its impact on teaching and learning outcomes, as well as its potential to improve motivation and interest among students and gamers.
